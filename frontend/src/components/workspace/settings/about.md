# 🦌 [About bookistudios AI 2.0](https://github.com/bytedance/bookistudios-ai)

> **From Open Source, Back to Open Source**

**bookistudios AI** (**D**eep **E**xploration and **E**fficient **R**esearch **Flow**) is a community-driven SuperAgent harness that researches, codes, and creates.
With the help of sandboxes, memories, tools and skills, it handles
different levels of tasks that could take minutes to hours.
